## Calculated Fields Created

The following calculated fields were created in Tableau to support dashboard analysis:

### 1. Profit Margin

**Formula:**
Profit / Sales

**Purpose:**
Measures the percentage of sales retained as profit. This helps identify highly profitable products, regions, and customer segments.

---

### 2. Cost

**Formula:**
Sales − Profit

**Purpose:**
Calculates the estimated cost associated with each order, allowing comparison between revenue and profitability.

---

### 3. Average Order Value (AOV)

**Formula:**
Total Sales / Number of Unique Orders

**Purpose:**
Measures the average revenue generated per customer order and helps evaluate customer purchasing behavior.

---

### 4. Return Rate

**Formula:**
Returned Orders / Total Orders

**Purpose:**
Measures the percentage of orders that were returned. A higher return rate may indicate quality issues, customer dissatisfaction, or operational problems.

---

### 5. Shipping Delay Bucket

**Logic:**

* Fast (0–2 Days)
* Medium (3–5 Days)
* Delayed (6+ Days)

**Purpose:**
Groups delivery performance into meaningful categories to identify shipping efficiency and potential logistics issues.

# Business Insights

## 1. Sales Trend

**Observation:** Sales fluctuate over time with some months showing significantly higher sales than others.

**Data Evidence:** The Sales Trend chart shows noticeable peaks and dips across different months.

**Business Interpretation:** Sales are influenced by seasonal demand and promotional periods.

**Recommended Action:** Investigate the causes of high-performing months and replicate successful sales strategies during lower-performing periods.

---

## 2. Regional Performance

**Observation:** The South region generates the highest sales compared to the other regions.

**Data Evidence:** The Regional Performance chart shows South leading in total sales.

**Business Interpretation:** The South region has stronger market demand or better operational performance.

**Recommended Action:** Continue investing in the South while identifying improvement opportunities in lower-performing regions.

---

## 3. Category Profitability

**Observation:** Technology products contribute the highest profit among all categories.

**Data Evidence:** The Category Profitability chart shows several Technology sub-categories with the highest profit values.

**Business Interpretation:** Technology products deliver better financial returns.

**Recommended Action:** Increase focus on high-margin Technology products while improving profitability in weaker categories.

---

## 4. Customer Segment Performance

**Observation:** The Home Office customer segment generates the highest sales.

**Data Evidence:** The Customer Segment chart shows Home Office leading in sales.

**Business Interpretation:** Home Office customers contribute significantly to business revenue.

**Recommended Action:** Develop targeted marketing campaigns and loyalty programs for this segment.

---

## 5. Shipping Performance

**Observation:** Standard Class has the longest average delivery time.

**Data Evidence:** The Shipping Performance chart shows Standard Class with the highest average delivery days.

**Business Interpretation:** Standard shipping may reduce customer satisfaction if delays increase.

**Recommended Action:** Improve logistics efficiency or encourage customers to choose faster shipping options.

---

## 6. Discount Impact

**Observation:** Higher discounts are generally associated with lower profits.

**Data Evidence:** The Discount vs Profit scatter plot shows profits decreasing as discount percentages increase.

**Business Interpretation:** Excessive discounting reduces profit margins.

**Recommended Action:** Optimize discount strategies by balancing customer attraction with profitability.

---

## 7. Return Pattern

**Observation:** Furniture has the highest return rate among product categories.

**Data Evidence:** The Return Analysis chart shows Furniture with the largest return rate.

**Business Interpretation:** Product quality, customer expectations, or shipping issues may contribute to higher returns.

**Recommended Action:** Investigate return reasons and implement corrective actions to reduce return rates.

---

## 8. Business Opportunity

**Observation:** Combining regional, category, and customer segment insights reveals opportunities for targeted business growth.

**Data Evidence:** The dashboard integrates sales, profit, shipping, discount, and return analyses into one view.

**Business Interpretation:** Decision-makers can identify profitable markets while minimizing operational risks.

**Recommended Action:** Continue monitoring these KPIs regularly and use dashboard insights to support strategic business decisions.
